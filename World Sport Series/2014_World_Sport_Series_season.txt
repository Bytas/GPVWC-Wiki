The '''2014 World Sport Series season''' will be the third [[Grand Prix Virtual World Championship|GPVWC]] [[World Sport Series]] season. The 2014 season will be the first World Sport Series season to use a different mod to that found in the inaugural season. However, the Porsche 911 remains as the series' sole vehicle.
